HOW TO COOK PORK SISIG


Here's a quick tutorial on how to cook one of the best Filipino dish that was ever created.

But first, you should know the ingredients for this perfect dish:

  • 1 kilo Pork Liempo
  • onion
  • 4 cloves of garlic
  • 3pcs green chili
  • 500ml of cooking oil
  • 2-3 tbsp of soy sauce
  • 1 sachet of oyster sauce
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup of mayonnaise (optional)
  • red chilis(optional)

STEP 1

Pour all your oil on a deep frying pan and wait for it to heat up.

STEP 2

When your oil is ready, put all the pork in the pan and cover it for 5 mins to prevent the oil from splattering.

STEP 3

While you're waiting for the pork to be cooked, mince your garlic and chop your onion into medium size.

STEP 4

After your pork gets crispy, let it rest for 2-3mins then chop it into bite sizes.

STEP 5

On a low heat, saute your onion, after 30 seconds put the garlic into the pan and saute it for another 30 sec.

STEP 6

Put your chopped pork liempo in the pan then pour your soy sauce and oyster sauce as well. When the flavor already infused the pork, turn off the stove and put your green and red chili, eggs, and mayonnaise (optional) and mix it together.

STEP 7

When everything is ready, prepare your plating and serve it with your rice.

And there you have it, your homemade pork sisig!
